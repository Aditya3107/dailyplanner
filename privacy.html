Daily Planner · Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

The short version

Daily Planner does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything you enter stays on your device.

What data the app handles

Your goals, tasks, completion history, streak, and settings (including your name, if you choose to enter one) are stored locally on your device using your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device. There is no account, no sign-in, and no server.

Analytics and tracking

The app contains no analytics, no advertising, no cookies, and no third-party trackers of any kind.

Backups

The optional "Export data" feature saves a backup file to your device at your request. You control that file entirely; it is never uploaded anywhere.

Deleting your data

Use "Reset everything" in the app's settings, uninstall the app, or clear your browser's site data. All of these permanently remove everything.

Contact

Questions or concerns: open an issue at github.com/Aditya3107/dailyplanner or visit adityaparikh.me.
